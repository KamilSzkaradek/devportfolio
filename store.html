Online Store

1.The store's main page consists of navigation bar at the top, Category bar at the left and the middle part where products from the database are displayed based on criteria (discounted at the top, random category in the middle, products for less than 100$ at the bottom).





2.Choosing a category from the bar on the left will display every product of that category.





3.You can also search products by their name by entering it in the Search bar above.





4.Cart's page.





5.You can add any products to the cart and create a new order by clicking the Order button (doesn't matter if you are logged in, however if you're not then that order just won't be assigned to any account).





6.Login page, if both Login and Password are the same as in the database then user will be able to view his account. Clicking REGISTER below it will redirect you to registration page.





7.Login, E-mail and Password are needed to create an account, of coutse they need to be unique in the database.





8.After user logs in they can check they Order history and cancel an order.





9.Also change their information.





10.If admin logs in he will be able to view everything from the database and be able to delete any item, product etc.





11.Admin can also add a new product.
